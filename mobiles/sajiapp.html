Saji - Aplikasi Restoran

Saji is an Application works both in Android & iOS. Saji is use to help Restaurant's Transactions Management like Order Process (from Waiter to Kitchen, Kitchen to Waiter, untill payment in Cashier), Cash Flow Management, and Reporting. Saji also can be use with multiple Restaurant's Outlet in one account.

Saji is developed with Flutter (Dart), a Framework from Google for Mobile Hybrid Application Development.
